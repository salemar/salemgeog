Homework 8: Spatial analysis

Salem Reaves


Buildings in the 100 ft. Critical Zone Buffer in Riviera Beach, Maryland.

The state of Maryland preserves a 100 ft. buffer along the bay's coastline. I was interested in zooming in on an area and looking at what is within that buffer.

While the buffer mostly consists of shrubs, trees, and grasses, there are areas with residential property within the buffer that contain lawns and buildings.

Geoprocessing:

1. Clipped the national coastlines with the Maryland state boundary to get a more precise bay coastline.

2. Buffer 100 ft. from coastline (calculated 100/364,000 to approximate feet into decimal degrees).

3. Clipped building footprints with buffer to highlight segments contained inside of buffer.

Data used for this project

National coastline 2022 TIGER/Line shapefile
Maryland state boundary from MD ArcGIS
Building footprints from MD ArcGIS
Information about Critical Area Buffer
